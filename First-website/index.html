My Favourite Things In Life

The world is a magical place, filled with many wonderful things.
Cute puppies, beautiful sunsets, roaring waves and live music, to name a few.
Of all the happy and beautiful moments life can offer, I am,
just like Amélie Poulain, particularly fond of the small enjoyments in life.

I love to experience places in a slow pace, for instance hiking or on horse back, where the goal isn´t reaching destinations, but the experience itself. The mind widens from being close to nature, getting to know people, trying new foods and ways of living.

Here is my Top 10 list of fabulous things in life:

  • Italian ice cream
  • Cats
  • The sound of the ocean
  • Forest walks
  • Hugs
  • Skinny dipping
  • Full moon in August
  • Melting butter on freshly baked bread
  • The soft touch of a horse mule
  • Acts of kindness from a stranger

N.B. Listed in no particular order.
